Why Donate Blood?

Every two seconds, someone needs blood. Whether it's for an accident victim, a cancer patient, or a child undergoing surgery, your donation can save up to three lives. Blood cannot be manufactured — it can only come from generous donors like you. Be a hero. Start with one drop.
